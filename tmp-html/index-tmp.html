Azul, Salut, Salam, Hello, Hallo !

My name is Adel Ferdjoukh (Ɛadel Ferǧux) and I am a researcher in computer science. I do my research in the context of Software Engineering, in particular, I am interested in Model Driven Engineering. Currently, I work as a postdoctoral researcher in TU Wien (The Technical University of Vienna, Austria).


Research topics

I do my research within the BIG research group in Vienna. My favorite topics are: Software engineering, Model Driven Engineering, Model Transformations Testing, Model generation.


Current project

Since summer 2018, I am involved in LEA xDSML research project.


Previous positions


Montpellier (2013-2016)

Between 2013 and 2016, I worked within the LIRMM research laboratory in the MAREL group. LIRMM belongs to CNRS (French Scientist Research Council) and the University of Montpellier, France. During these 3 years, I worked on GRIMM project (Generation of Instances of Meta-Models).


Nantes (2016-2018)

In 2016, I moved to the University of Nantes where I worked for 2 years as non-permanent Teacher/Researcher. My research group was named Naomod (former Atlanmod). It belongs to LS2N research lab and Mines Telecom of Nantes. In Nantes, I worked on the combination of testing and verification for ensuring the quality of model transformations (veriatl project), the diversity in MDE (COMODI project) and the static debugging of meta-models (TIWIZI project).
